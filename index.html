Signature Collections

01

Carrara White Marble

The epitome of luxury, Carrara marble features delicate grey veining on a pristine white background. Perfect for countertops, flooring, and statement walls.

  • Origin: Tuscany, Italy
  • Finish: Polished, Honed, Brushed
  • Application: Interior & Exterior
02

Black Galaxy Granite

Stunning black granite with golden speckles that shimmer like stars. Highly durable and perfect for high-traffic areas and kitchen countertops.

  • Origin: Andhra Pradesh, India
  • Finish: Polished, Flamed
  • Application: Countertops, Flooring
03

Teakwood Sandstone

Warm, earthy tones with natural wood-like patterns. Ideal for creating rustic and contemporary outdoor spaces with superior weather resistance.

  • Origin: Rajasthan, India
  • Finish: Natural, Sandblasted
  • Application: Outdoor Paving, Cladding
