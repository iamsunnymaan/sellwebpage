Our Story

About LITHOS

LITHOS is a premier luxury stone manufacturer and supplier, bringing over two decades of expertise in crafting exquisite natural stone products. From our private quarries to your architectural masterpiece, we ensure every piece meets the highest standards of quality and elegance.

20+ Years

Of Excellence in Stone Craftsmanship

5000+ Projects

Delivered Worldwide

50+ Countries

Global Export Network

Our advanced manufacturing facility uses Italian precision machinery to transform premium marble, sandstone, limestone, and granite into exceptional stone creations. We follow sustainable sourcing and eco-friendly production practices, while our skilled artisans and designers bring refined stone solutions to life for luxury homes, hotels, and commercial spaces.
