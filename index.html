Our Story

About LITHOS

LITHOS is a premier luxury stone manufacturer and supplier, bringing over two decades of expertise in crafting exquisite natural stone products. From our private quarries to your architectural masterpiece, we ensure every piece meets the highest standards of quality and elegance.

20+ Years

Of Excellence in Stone Craftsmanship

5000+ Projects

Delivered Worldwide

50+ Countries

Global Export Network

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are equipped with Italian precision machinery, allowing us to transform raw stone into breathtaking works of art. We specialize in marble, sandstone, limestone, and granite products that elevate spaces from ordinary to extraordinary.

With a commitment to sustainability, we source responsibly and implement eco-friendly practices throughout our production process. Our team of skilled artisans and designers work tirelessly to bring your vision to life, whether it's a grand hotel lobby, a luxury residence, or a commercial space.
