Care & Maintenance

Daily Care:

  • Clean with soft, damp cloth
  • Use pH-neutral stone cleaner
  • Avoid acidic or abrasive cleaners

Periodic Maintenance:

  • Seal stone surface annually
  • Professional cleaning every 2-3 years
  • Inspect for cracks or damage regularly

Protection Tips:

  • Use coasters under glasses and cups
  • Protect from extreme temperature changes
  • Clean spills immediately to prevent staining
